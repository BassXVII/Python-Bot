  Ashley Nicolette Frangipane  /ˌfrændʒɪˈpɑːni/; born September 29, 1994 , known professionally as Halsey  /ˈhɔːlzi/ , is an American singer, songwriter, and actress. Her stage name is a reference to the Halsey Street station of the New York City Subway in Brooklyn, and an anagram of her first name.
